Wellington


The image you see above is Wellington. Wellington is the capital of New Zealand located in the bottom of the north island. Its home to about 400K ish people. Wellington is known for the parliment and Beehive, which is where laws are made and how the country is managed. Wellington region has an area of around 8KM squared, but a city area of around 444KM squared.

Haka


This image is the haka, a cultural performance of New Zealand. Hakas are often performed to introduce new people to a place. It consists of both men and woman. It is also performend in national sports teams. As you can see, the All Blacks are performing the haka in the image above at a rugby world cup.

Southern Alps


The image above is the Southern Alps in New Zealand. This is a mountain range that stretches from Nelson all the way to the bottom of the South Island. It is famous for its stunning landscapes. Since the Southern Alps stretches across the entire South Island, so it stretches for about 500km. These mountains could also be used for snowing and skiing during the winters.

Marae


This is a Marae. A Marae is a sacred building in Maori culture, and it belongs to an iwi, hapu or whanau. But nowdays its considered a place of belonging, where people gather for celebrations, ceremonies and community events. It consists of a meeting house and a dinning room.
