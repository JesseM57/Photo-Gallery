MLB


This picture you see above is the main logo of Major League Baseball. Major League Baseball is one of the most famous baseball assotiations in the world. It has around 30 baseball teams from USA and 1 from Canada. MLB is split into 2 leagues. American and National league. Each league is then split into pools, such as national east, national west and national central. The same applies for the American League. The best players from each league is selected midway during the season to form an all star team. 2 all star teams play each other which is known as MLB All Star Game. During the late time of the season, the World Series is played. Which contains the best team of National and American League.

WBC


This picture above shows the WBC 2026 finals that happened a while ago. The 2026 WBC took place in March 2026. It contained 4 pools, A B C and D. Each pool had 5 teams. This picture shows team Venezuela VS team USA playing each other at Miami, Florida. The 2 teams made it to the finals after pool plays, quaterfinals and semifinals.

WBSC U15


The picture you see above is the main logo for WBSC U15 Baseball World Cup. This tournament is held every 2 years in around November. For example, 2024 WBSC U15 Baseball World Cup. In 2024, the tournement was held in Italy, and it will be the same for 2026. Teams can qualify by winning regional tournements against other countries. For example, Australia won the Oceania Cup held in Guam. The qualified teams then play each other in the World Cup to determine a champion.
